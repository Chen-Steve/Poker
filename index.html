Game Rules

How to Play

Welcome to Blackjack! Here's how you can play:

  • Start by placing your bet.
  • Click 'Deal' to receive your cards.
  • Choose 'Hit' to get another card or 'Stand' to hold your total.
  • The goal is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Gameplay

After receiving your two cards:

  1. Hit: If you want another card to try and get closer to 21, click 'Hit'.
  2. Stand: Satisfied with your hand? Click 'Stand' and it's the dealer's turn.
  3. Double Down: Feel confident? Double your bet for one more card.

Betting and Payouts

  • Wins pay 1:1; Blackjack wins pay 3:2.
  • If you lose, you lose your bet amount.
  • A draw returns your bet.
